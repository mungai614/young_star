Organization Rules & Guidelines

Contributions to the organization are non-refundable. Members are expected to honor their commitments.

No member may receive a loan exceeding the percentage of their contributions. This ensures fairness for all members.

Loans are subject to an interest rate of 10%. For example, a repayment of 500 will include an additional 10% interest.

Members must attend all scheduled meetings. Absences without a valid reason will incur a fine of 50 shillings.

Being late for meetings will result in a fine of 20 shillings.

A registration fee of 100 shillings must be paid by every new member.

The organization operates independently. Personal disputes must not interfere with organizational activities.

Members must treat each other respectfully. Harassment, discrimination, or misconduct is strictly prohibited.

All contributions and loans must be properly recorded. Members are accountable for the funds they handle.

Members must comply with all organizational rules and decisions. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.
Note: These rules ensure fairness, transparency, and smooth operation. All members are encouraged to read and follow them carefully.
